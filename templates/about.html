About Us

A few words about us

A student initiative to help farmers across the country do better with their produce so as to not have any losses and increase their income

Modern technologies have allowed us to keep up with the demand for food for 7 billion people. However food security is still threatened by a number of factors like climate change, plant diseases and reduction in pollinators. Plant diseases are not only a threat to the food security at a global level but also impact the livelihoods of the small-scale farmers. For many years, there have been various efforts to alleviate crop diseases and improve the quality and quantity of the production. Our project aims to help reduce these problems by consolidating all the required modules such as Weather Forecasting, Leaf Disease Detection, Fruit Quality Prediction and Market Price Tracker which will give the farmers a one stop solution to the difficulties they face. The Market Price Tracker will allow the farmers to keep a check on the market prices which will help them in selling their produce more effectively and increase their profits.

Objectives

To support small-scale and large-scale farmers alike by simplifying the crop-monitoring and quality management process, along with convenient access to expert advice via a consolidated hassle-free portal. To enable easy viewing of the results of: Plant Leaf disease monitoring Fruit Quality monitoring Soil quality monitoring Weather Predictions and Forecasts (Rainfall/drought/flood/Extreme weather events) Real time analysis of Market prices for popular and in-demand crops To perform optimized abstraction of the backend and simplify the frontend to the maximum extent possible.

About

A student initiative to make lives better for farmers by creating a system that allows farmers to monitor their crops regularly and informs them of all the possible diseases in order to take action early and avoid losses of produce and income
